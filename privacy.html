Plain English summary: We collect only what we need to run ReplyDrop. We never sell your data. The Chrome extension reads email content only to generate replies — that content is never stored. You can delete your account at any time.

01

Information We Collect

We collect the minimum information necessary to provide the ReplyDrop service:

  • Email address — used to create your account, send sign-in codes, and identify your subscription status.
  • Subscription & billing data — your plan (Free or Pro) and Stripe customer ID. We do not store full credit card numbers; payments are handled entirely by Stripe.
  • Usage data — number of replies generated per day (for Free plan limits). We do not log the content of messages you paste or the replies we generate.
  • Contact messages — if you submit a support request via our contact form, we store your name, email, and message.

We do not collect passwords (we use passwordless magic-link / OTP authentication), browsing history, or any information beyond what is listed above.

02

How We Use Your Information

Your information is used exclusively to:

  • Authenticate you and maintain your account session
  • Determine your plan and enforce usage limits
  • Process payments and manage your subscription via Stripe
  • Respond to support requests you submit
  • Send transactional emails (sign-in codes, billing receipts) — no marketing emails without your explicit consent

We do not sell, rent, or share your personal information with third parties for advertising or marketing purposes.

03

Chrome Extension Data Practices

The ReplyDrop Chrome Extension accesses the content of emails and messages you are actively viewing in order to generate reply suggestions. Here is exactly how that works:

  • What we read: The text content of the email or message currently open in your browser tab (Gmail, Outlook Web, Yelp, Google Reviews, or Facebook).
  • When we read it: Only when you click the ReplyDrop button and request a reply. We do not passively scan or monitor your inbox.
  • What we do with it: The message text is sent to our backend API, which passes it to the Claude AI model (by Anthropic) to generate reply options. It is then immediately discarded — we do not store, log, or retain email content.
  • What we store locally: Your email address and Pro status are stored in Chrome's local extension storage so you stay signed in between sessions. This data never leaves your device except when verifying your account with our server.

We never read your inbox passively. The extension only accesses content when you explicitly activate it on a specific email or message. No background scanning. No data collection without your action.

04

Data Storage & Security

Your account data is stored in Supabase, a SOC 2 Type II certified database platform hosted on AWS infrastructure. We use the following security practices:

  • All data is encrypted in transit via TLS/HTTPS
  • Database access is restricted to authenticated service roles only
  • Authentication uses Supabase Auth with one-time passcodes — no passwords are ever stored
  • Payment data is handled exclusively by Stripe (PCI DSS Level 1 certified)

While we implement industry-standard security measures, no method of transmission over the internet is 100% secure. We encourage you to contact us immediately if you suspect any unauthorized access to your account.

05

Third-Party Services

ReplyDrop uses the following third-party services to operate:

  • Supabase — user authentication and database storage. Privacy Policy
  • Stripe — payment processing and subscription management. Privacy Policy
  • Anthropic (Claude API) — AI reply generation. Message content sent for generation is subject to Anthropic's data handling policies. Privacy Policy
  • Render — cloud hosting for our backend API. Privacy Policy

Each of these providers has their own privacy policies governing how they handle data passed to them.

06

Data Retention

We retain your account data (email, plan status) for as long as your account is active. If you request account deletion, we will remove your personal data within 30 days.

Support messages submitted via our contact form are retained for up to 2 years to assist with follow-up inquiries.

We do not retain any email or message content you submit for reply generation — it is processed in real time and immediately discarded.

07

Your Rights

Depending on your location, you may have the following rights regarding your personal data:

  • Access — request a copy of the data we hold about you
  • Correction — request that we correct inaccurate data
  • Deletion — request that we delete your account and associated data
  • Portability — request your data in a portable format
  • Opt-out — opt out of any non-essential communications

To exercise any of these rights, email us at support@replydrop.ai. We will respond within 30 days.

08

Children's Privacy

ReplyDrop is not directed at children under the age of 13. We do not knowingly collect personal information from children under 13. If you believe we have inadvertently collected information from a child under 13, please contact us immediately and we will delete it promptly.

09

Changes to This Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. When we make material changes, we will update the "Last updated" date at the top of this page. For significant changes, we may also notify you via email.

Your continued use of ReplyDrop after any changes constitutes your acceptance of the updated policy.
